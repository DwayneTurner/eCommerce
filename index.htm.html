Jordan 1s Mid New Love 2017 Who here has the Air Jordan 1 Old Love, New Love Pack from back in the day? Jordan Brand released those two shoes as a pack 10 years ago. This means that if you wanted one, you also had to buy the other. Now, it looks like Jordan Brand is bringing back one-half of the pack, the Air Jordan 1 Mid New Love. Originally released back in 2007, the Air Jordan 1 Mid New Love represents Michael Jordan’s love for motorcycle racing.
It is made out of leather in a Yellow (Varsity Maize) and Black color scheme. The 2017 version comes with a new feature on the upper. Instead of coming with a jeweled Wings branding on the ankle, Jordan Brand opts to give it a more traditional look by replacing it with a stamped/painted on finish. Jumpman branding on the tongue, two set of laces (Yellow and Black), and a White midsole and Yellow translucent outsole round out the look of the shoe. Expect these to release sometime this month. We will provide the exact release date as soon as it’s made public.

Jordan 12s French Blues
The Air Jordan 12 French Blue may not be an OG colorway, but many adore it more than classic iterations of the silhouette. Last seen in 2004, the popular finish is finally scheduled to receive its first retro treatment after twelve years, as the model celebrates its 20th anniversary in 2016. Maintaining its distinctive palette overall, the silhouette is comprised of White tumbled leather, while French Blue overlays are prominent on its forefoot, outsole unit and rear portion. Metallic Silver accents provided by the top eyelets, Black Jumpman branding on the tongue and subtle Grey panels on the sides consist as notable contrasting elements.
